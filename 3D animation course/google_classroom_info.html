About Google Classroom

Google Classroom is the central hub for all course announcements, assignments, resources, and communication for the 3D Animations course. Make sure to check it regularly to stay up to date!

Why We Use Google Classroom

  • Easy access to assignments and deadlines.
  • Centralized location for course materials and resources.
  • Direct communication with your instructor and classmates.
  • Turn in your work and receive feedback efficiently.

How to Join

Follow these steps to join our Google Classroom for the 3D Animations course.

Joining Instructions

  • Go to classroom.google.com.
  • Sign in with your school Google account.
  • Click the + button in the top right and select Join class.
  • Enter the class code: li2mvil
  • Or use this direct link: Join 3D Animations Classroom

Tips & Etiquette

Make the most of Google Classroom by following these best practices.

Best Practices

  • Check the Classroom stream and your email regularly for updates.
  • Turn in assignments before the deadline.
  • Use the comments section for questions related to assignments.
  • Be respectful and professional in all communications.
  • Organize your files and keep track of feedback.
